Who We Are

About Elara Path

Elara Path Rehabilitation is a planned 20–30 bed luxury residential substance use disorder center in Frisco, Colorado. We combine evidence-based addiction treatment, dual-diagnosis psychiatry, and trauma-informed care with five-star hospitality. We serve high-functioning professionals, executives, and individuals who demand privacy, discretion, and individualized care.

Evidence-Based Treatment

Licensed clinicians delivering modalities grounded in research and measurable outcomes.

Boutique 24-Bed Setting

Intimate scale means tailored attention, calm energy, and a restorative sense of space.

Physician and Psychiatric Oversight

Medical leadership for detox, medication management, and integrated mental health care.

Clinical Programs

Clinical Programs

A continuum of care designed for lasting recovery.

Medical Detox (On-Site & Partnered)

On-site detox for low- to moderate-risk clients, with partner hospitals for higher acuity cases.

Residential Treatment

30–60 day stays, individual therapy, group therapy, psychiatry, and holistic services.

Dual-Diagnosis & Trauma Care

Integrated treatment for substance use, anxiety, depression, and trauma.

Aftercare & Alumni Support

Telehealth groups, coaching, and ongoing support for at least 12 months.
